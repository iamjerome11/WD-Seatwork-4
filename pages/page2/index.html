Engine

Luxury car engines usually have larger displacements (often ranging from 3.0 to 6.0 liters) and high power outputs, often exceeding 300 horsepower. This ensures rapid acceleration and robust performance across a range of driving conditions.

Handle

The handle of luxury cars typically refers to the door handle or grab handle of high-end vehicles. They also include advanced technology such as chrome, polished, aluminum, or leather.

Shifter

The shifter of luxury car is a central components in the vehicle interior, designed to enhance both functionality and aesthetics.
